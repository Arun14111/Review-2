7 Habits Of highly Effective People

Stephen R.Covey

It is a widely acclaimed guide that unveils a holistic approach to personal and professional development. Through timeless principles and actionable strategies, this influential book empowers individuals to cultivate effective habits, attain their goals, and live a purpose-driven life.

To Kill a Mockingbird

Harper Lee

Set in the 1930s, this classic novel explores themes of racial injustice and moral growth through the eyes of Scout, a young girl in a small Southern town.

The Alchemist

Paulo Coelho

This enchanting tale follows a young shepherd named Santiago on his quest for treasure, teaching readers valuable lessons about following their dreams and finding their true purpose in life.

1984

George Orwell

A dystopian masterpiece, Orwell's novel depicts a totalitarian society where Big Brother surveils every move, raising questions about individual freedom, government control, and the power of language.

Pride and Prejudice

Jane Austen

Austen's beloved classic portrays the complex romance between spirited Elizabeth Bennet and the aloof Mr. Darcy, offering a social commentary on love, class, and the role of women in 19th-century England.

Ponniyin Selvan

Kalki Krishnamurthy

A historical novel set in the Chola dynasty, it follows the life and adventures of the protagonist Vandiyathevan, intertwining politics, romance, and intrigue.

Sivagamiyin Sabatham

Kalki Krishnamurthy

Another historical novel by Kalki Krishnamurthy, it narrates the story of Sivagami, a brave and virtuous queen who played a significant role during the Pallava dynasty.

Kadal Pura

Sandilyan

A captivating historical novel that unfolds during the Chola period, it revolves around the life of the protagonist, Raja Raja Chola I, showcasing his military conquests and love affairs.

Sila Nerangalil Sila Manitharga

Jayakanthan

It delves into the journey of a young woman named Janaki, who grapples with the complexities of societal expectations, gender discrimination, and the pursuit of self-discovery.
